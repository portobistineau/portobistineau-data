FAQ

Do you sell minnows (or any other item)?

Unfortunately, our store is currently closed. We do have some crappie jigs available by appointment only, however. Please call or text for information.

Do you have cabins for rent?

Unfortunately, we do not. The Louisiana State Park has some cabins, please check their website for availability. Also, we are located 20 minutes from Minden, Louisiana, where several hotels are available.

Do you rent boats?

We do not. Sorry.

Can I launch during the drawdown periods?

Yes! We have our primary concrete launch on the north side and a gentler sloping gravel one on the south side. However, during full drawdown, you have to be more careful on the south end due to dropoffs. Contact me directly for more information on that.

How much is the launch fee?

The launch fee is $5.00. We have pay boxes at each of our launches. We also have electronic payment options with Venmo or Paypal. See the signs at the boxes.
